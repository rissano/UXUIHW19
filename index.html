About Me

Coming from a background in personal training, where effective communication and personalized solutions were key, and experience in hotel management, where attention to detail and customer satisfaction were paramount, Marissa brings a unique perspective to UX design, focusing on user-centered solutions that blend functionality with creativity.

My UX Skills

UI/Visual Design

My skills encompass color theory, typography, and layout design to ensure that every element on the screen is both functional and visually appealing. I aim to deliver seamless and delightful user experiences that enhance overall usability.

UX Design

Crafting intuitive and engaging user journeys through meticulous research, usability testing, and iterative design processes is how I ensure optimal user satisfaction and product success.

Prototyping

Prototyping is how my ideas come to life. Ranging from low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity, interactive models, they ensure a clear vision of the final product and enable effective communication and feedback throughout the design process.
